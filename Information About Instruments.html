Information About Various Musical Instruments

God's Greatest Gift: Music

Classical music not only nurtures our soul but grows our mind in ways that have been substantiated by countless studies over recent years. Indian Classical Music has two major traditions: the North Indian Classical Music known as Hindustani, and the South Indian Classical Music known as Carnatic. It was closely connected to nature taking inspiration from natural phenomena including the seasons and times of the day to create 'ragas' or musical moods and many time cycles or 'taals' that have been further codified.



Instruments typically used in Classical Music include Tabla, Harmonium, Veena, Tanpura, Bansuri, Shehnai etc. Let's check out some more details about these Musical Instruments.
