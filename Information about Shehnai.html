Information about Shehnai







Shehnai is a reed instrument very similar western oboe. Its sound has a quite powerful and has a districtive nasal quality. It is produced by blowing the reeds, which are bound together and held between the lips with a certain tension. The pitch can be altered by changing the pressure of lips in the air.

It is made of wood, except for a flaring metal ball attached to the bottom of the instrument, and measures about 12-20 inches in length, with six to eight keyless finger holes along ts body.

Some of the famous Shehnai players include Bismillah Khan, Anant Lal, Lokesh Anand and S Ballesh.
