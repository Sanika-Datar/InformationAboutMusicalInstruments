Information about Veena





Veena is one of the most ancient string instruments of India. It comprises a family of chordophone instruments from the India subcontinent. Ancient musical instruments evovled into many variations, such as lutes, zithers and arched harps. Many regional designs have different names such as the Rudra Veena, the Saraswati Veena, the Vichitra Veena etc.

It is 1.5m long and is made from jackwood. It has a large, round body, with a thick, wide neck, the end of which is carved into the head of a dragon. A small resonator is attached to the underside of the neck. It has 24 metal frets embedded in hardened bees-wax. mixed with charcoal powder.

Some of the famous Veena players include Rajhesh Vaidya, Jayanthi Kumaresh, Ustad Asad Ali Khan and Jyoti Hedge.
